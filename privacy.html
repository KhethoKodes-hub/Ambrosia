Our Commitment to Your Privacy

LU Ally's is committed to protecting your personal information in compliance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) of South Africa.

What Information We Collect

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Booking details and accommodation preferences
  • Any communication or correspondence with us

Why We Collect It

  • To fulfill transport and accommodation bookings
  • To communicate with you and respond to queries
  • To meet legal and tax obligations

Your Rights Under POPIA

  • You can request access to your personal data
  • You can ask us to correct or delete your data
  • You can object to how we use your information

How We Protect Your Data

We store personal data securely and do not share it with third parties unless required by law or necessary for service delivery (e.g. invoicing or compliance).

Cookie Usage

Our website may use cookies for performance and analytics. You can control this via your browser settings.
