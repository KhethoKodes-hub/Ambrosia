Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

We primarily serve the Northern Cape province with transportation services extending to various provinces across South Africa. Our main operations are centered around Postmasburg, Kathu, Kimberley, and surrounding mining regions.

You can make a reservation by calling us at 074 224 3219, emailing bookings@ambrosialogistics.co.za, or using the contact form on this page. We recommend booking in advance, especially during peak mining seasons.

Our fleet includes site tipping trucks, yellow fleet equipment (777 trucks, FEL, graders, dozers, diesel bowsers, water trucks, TLBs), passenger transport vehicles (9-21 seaters), and light commercial vehicles. Visit our Fleet page for more details.

Yes, we offer both short-term and long-term accommodation options. Our long-term stays include bachelor units and 2-bedroom family units with secure parking. Special rates are available for extended stays. Visit our Accommodation page for more information.

We offer 24/7 emergency transport services for urgent requirements. Response times vary based on location and current availability, but we prioritize emergency requests and typically can deploy vehicles within 2-4 hours for local requests.
