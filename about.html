Our Story

From humble beginnings to trusted service provider in the Northern Cape

Built on Excellence

Founded in 2013 by Alison Neels, LU Ally's began as a small transport operation serving local mining companies in Postmasburg. Recognizing the need for both reliable transport and quality accommodation in the area, Alison expanded the business to include guesthouse services.

What started as a single tipper truck has grown into a multi-service company with a diverse fleet and luxury accommodation options. Our journey reflects our commitment to excellence and our deep understanding of our clients' needs in the Tsantsabane area.

Our Mission

To be the leading female-owned transport and accommodation provider in the Northern Cape by delivering reliable, safe, and cost-effective services that exceed client expectations.

Our Vision

To transform the transport and hospitality landscape through innovative solutions while empowering our community and maintaining the highest standards of service.
