About Ambrosia Logistics

Delivering excellence in transportation and accommodation since 2013

Our Story

Founded in 2013 by Alison Neels, Ambrosia Logistics began with a vision to provide quality, low-cost accommodation and transport services in the Tsantsabane area.

What started as a small local operation has grown into a comprehensive logistics and accommodation provider serving clients across multiple provinces. Our journey has been marked by consistent growth, an expanding fleet, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Today, as a 100% black woman-owned company, we take pride in our ability to adapt to market needs while maintaining the personal touch that has defined our service from the beginning.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To be the best female-owned company in the transport industry by anticipating client demands and providing cost-effective solutions in transporting goods and accommodation services.

Our Vision

To constantly exceed client expectations by attaining a standard of excellence in transporting goods and accommodation services, becoming partners rather than just service providers.

Our Journey

2013

Founded by Alison Neels with a focus on local transportation and guesthouse services in Postmasburg.

2015

Expanded our fleet to include site tipping trucks and secured our first major mining contract.

2017

Added yellow fleet equipment and began offering comprehensive mining support services.

2019

Expanded accommodation services with additional guesthouse facilities and long-term stay options.

2021

Achieved BBBEE Level 1 certification and expanded operations to multiple provinces.

2023

Celebrated 10 years of excellence in logistics and accommodation services with an expanded fleet and client base.
