Our Guesthouse

Experience comfort and convenience at Ambrosia Logistics Guesthouse

Since 2013, we've been providing affordable, comfortable accommodation for visitors to Postmasburg and the surrounding areas. Our guesthouse offers a perfect blend of home-like comfort and hotel-like amenities, making it the ideal choice for business travelers, contractors, and tourists alike.

Located just a short walk from the Sibilos shopping mall, our guesthouse provides easy access to local amenities while offering a peaceful retreat after a long day.
