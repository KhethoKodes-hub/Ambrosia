Our Story

Since 2013, Ambrosia Logistics has been providing exceptional transportation and accommodation services in the Northern Cape and beyond.

Founded by Alison Neels, we recognized the need for quality, low-cost accommodation and transport services in the Tsantsabane area. Today, we take pride in being a 100% black woman-owned company offering multi-unit commodity carriers and luxury accommodation.

Our commitment to on-time service, reliability, and safe delivery has remained unchanged throughout our growth. We're fully licensed and compliant with all regulatory requirements.

Site Tipping Trucks

Specializing in site tipper transportation with a diversified fleet that includes specialty trailers and vans to move various unique commodities.
